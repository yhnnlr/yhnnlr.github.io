Mini-Golf
Solo Project
Mini-Game / C / CSFML
2 weeks (in our free time) - July 2022
Mini-Golf is, you guessed it, a mini-golf game. However the rules are a little bit different, you need to put the ball in the hole without touching any obstacle!
This small project was a challenge from our math teacher: originally, we'd been asked to bounce the ball off all sorts of convex surfaces, however we did not have the math knowledge necessary at the time to make this possible. So, in my version, you have to dodge those shapes!
Software used : Visual Studio, CSFML, Tortoise SVN
Skills involved : Collisions detection, Physics
