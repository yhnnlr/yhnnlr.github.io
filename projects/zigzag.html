ZigZag
Solo Project
Arcade Game / C# / Unity
4 hours - November 2024
ZigZag is the first in the series of games I made in 4 hours. It's a replica of the eponymous game ZigZag by Ketchapp.
Making a functional game in such a short period of time was a challenge, but it was fun nonetheless. I'm happy with the result.
Software used : Visual Studio, Unity, Tortoise SVN
Skills involved : Prototyping, Gameplay Programming, UI
