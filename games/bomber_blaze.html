Bomber Blaze
Super! Game Corp
Trailer
Bomberman-like / C / CSFML
1 week - 2022
Bomber Blaze is a Bomberman-like in a pixel-art style. Kill the enemies and find the exit of each randomly-generated level!
This was our first project on CSFML, and the preliminary project for Hematomancer. We worked on it 1 day a week for 5 weeks with a team of 6 programmers and 3 graphists.
I worked on the player's bombs, the map generation and helped on the UI.
I don't think much of this project, it's a really small game and everybody did what they were asked to do.
Software used : Visual Studio, CSFML, Google Docs, Tortoise SVN
Skills involved : Gameplay Programming, UI, Teamwork
