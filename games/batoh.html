B.A.T.O.H
Smooth Brain Studio
Source code
Auto-Battler / C# / Unity
10 days - 2023
B.A.T.O.H is an auto-battler playable on mobile and PC, where you play as the captain of a fleet of ships. You'll have to buy different boats from different eras with unique abilities, which you'll have to meticulously position in order to defeat your opponents.
You can play solo in Campaign mode, or in online multiplayer in Versus mode. Assemble a fleet of ships and establish the best possible strategy to prove you're the best captain on these seas!
B.A.T.O.H was our first group project on Unity. We worked on it 1 day a week for 10 weeks with a team of 7 programmers and 4 graphists.
I joined the project halfway through its development with a new team, so I didn't do anything major. I helped program the boats' abilities, and I also worked a little on the UI.
Software used : Visual Studio, Unity, Trello, Github Desktop
Skills involved : Gameplay Programming, UI, Object-Oriented Programming, Teamwork
