Party Game / C++ / SFML
9 days - 2022
Quiz Fish is a cartoon-like Party Game in which you'll challenge your friends or family around a quiz on various topics. Answer right to get a chance to fish in the pond!

Language: French only!
This was our first SFML project as a group. We worked on it 1 day a week for 9 weeks with a team of 6 programmers and 4 graphists.
I personnaly worked on the game's architecture, the game design, the fishing minigame and implementing the UI. I even made some assets myself.
This was my introduction to making larger games with C++. We may have had problems dividing up the tasks, which left me with the majority of the game to code.
Software used : Visual Studio, SFML, Trello, Tortoise SVN
Skills involved : Gameplay Programming, Game Design, UI, Object-Oriented Programming, Teamwork, Architecture, Leadership
